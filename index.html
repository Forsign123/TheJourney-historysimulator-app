The Journey: The Endeavour Voyage in Real-Time

A unique historical project by Codesign Projects.

What is The Journey?

The Journey is an Android application that allows you to experience Captain James Cook's first voyage (1768–1771) exactly as it happened, in true real-time. When you start the app, you begin a three-year journey where notifications and log entries arrive on your phone at the exact moment they were recorded over 250 years ago.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How does the real-time aspect work?

The app synchronizes with the historical timeline. If the HMS Endeavour spent two weeks at sea without sight of land, the app will remain quiet, simulating the isolation of 18th-century sailing.

What logs are included?

The app features the verbatim journals of Captain James Cook and Joseph Banks, alongside modern nautical commentary to help explain 18th-century maritime terms.

Is there a map?

Yes. The app includes a live map tracking the ship's coordinates, using historical data to interpolate the ship's position even on days when logs weren't recorded.
