The Real-Time Historical Adventure

Follow the wake of the HMS Endeavour as it happened, 250+ years later.

Experience History in Real-Time

The Journey is a high-fidelity simulator that tracks Captain James Cook’s epic voyage of discovery. Unlike a static history book, this app syncs with the historical calendar, delivering verbatim log entries exactly when they were written.

Verbatim Log Entries

Read the actual remarks and observations from the logs of 1768. From the fitting of the ship at Deptford Yard to the crossing of the Equator.

Interpolated Navigation

Our algorithm uses historical wind, speed, and context to provide a continuous GPS track of the ship's location, even where coordinates weren't recorded.

Immersive Nautical Interface

The Nautical Theme

Switch to Parchment Mode for an authentic 18th-century feel. Features include:

  • Ambient Ship Sounds (Creaking wood, wind, and waves)
  • Nautical Typography
  • Interactive 18th-century Style Maps

The Modern Explorer

Toggle to Modern Dark Mode for high-contrast reading during night-time exploration. Includes global offline map support for tracking anywhere.

App Features & Expedition Notes

Voyage Date: Starts July 27, 1768 (Real-time Sync)
Offline Maps: Global zoom levels 0-5 available offline.
Expedition Notes: Glossary, Crew Manifest, Image Gallery, and Navigation Methods.
Accessibility: Adjustable text sizes (Small, Medium, Large).
