# ⚓ The Journey: Captain Cook’s Journal
> **The most epic real-time historical simulator of discovery.**

"The Journey" is an immersive mobile experience that allows you to follow Captain James Cook’s 1768 voyage aboard the HMS Endeavour in **true real-time**. This isn't just a digital book; it is a historical companion that syncs with the calendar, delivering verbatim log entries and navigation data exactly as they were written over 250 years ago.

## 🌟 Key Features

### ⏳ Real-Time Synchronization
Experience the voyage as it happened. The app tracks the daily progression of the 1768 expedition. If Cook was battling a storm in the Atlantic on this specific calendar date, you will receive that log entry today.

### 📜 Verbatim Historical Logs
Read the actual remarks, observations, and descriptions directly from Captain Cook’s personal journals. 
* **Historical Accuracy:** Includes entries from the fitting of the ship at **Deptford Yard** (July 1768) to the crossing of the Equator and beyond.

### 🗺️ Intelligent Navigation Tracking
Since 18th-century logs didn't provide minute-by-minute GPS coordinates, our engine **painstakingly interpolates** the ship's location based on:
* Historical recorded speed and wind direction.
* Journal context and coastal landmarks.
* Modern determination (e.g., *32° 38' North, 16° 54' West*).

### 🎧 Immersive Atmosphere
* **Ambient Ship Sounds:** Toggleable background audio featuring creaking wood, wind through the rigging, and crashing waves.
* **Dual Themes:** Switch between the "Nautical" parchment style and a "Modern" dark interface.
* **Offline Maps:** Global voyage tracking works without an internet connection (Zoom levels 0-5).

## 🛠️ App Structure & Content

The app includes a comprehensive **Expedition Notes** suite:
* **Voyage Summary:** A bird's-eye view of the expedition's progress.
* **Navigation Methods:** Learn how 18th-century sailors used sextants and stars.
* **Crew Manifest:** Profiles of the sailors and scientists on board.
* **Glossary:** Definitions of archaic nautical terms (e.g., *Bason, Pendant, Deptford Yard*).
* **Image Gallery:** Historical sketches and modern recreations of the voyage.
